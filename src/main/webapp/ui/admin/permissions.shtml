Staff Permissions

Opus currently offers four user roles:

  • Department Administrator (staff person with academic personnel responsibilities at department level);
  • School Administrator (staff person with academic personnel duties in a dean's office);
  • Chair (faculty chair or director of an academic unit);
  • Dean (faculty dean, vice dean, or associate dean of a school).
Faculty users are not included in the current version of Opus (except for faculty with chair/director/dean duties).

Please contact the academic personnel coordinator in your dean's office to authorize your access request. Requests from dean's offices should be sent to opushelp@ucla.edu.
